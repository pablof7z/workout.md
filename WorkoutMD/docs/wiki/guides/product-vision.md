# Product Vision

## Vision

Workout.md is a minimal-friction workout tracker where fast tracking is the product wedge and AI is a planning layer behind the product, not a chatbot. The app is ADHD/2e-friendly without branding itself as such. <!-- [^42f3c-73b35] -->

## MVP Scope

The MVP must-haves are fast structured tracking, planned-vs-actual, clean Markdown output, basic plan create/adjust/repair, configurable coach, goals and history feeding planning, strength/hypertrophy support, and no gamification. <!-- [^42f3c-511b5] -->

## Core Loop

The core loop is: open → next set is ready → log what happened fast → deviations captured as normal → workout ends → coach quietly repairs tomorrow → close. <!-- [^42f3c-fea94] -->

## Emotional Tone

The app's emotional tone is neutral competence — no streaks, no badges, no shame, and a missed week produces zero red UI. <!-- [^42f3c-29b27] -->
