# OnboardingView

A calm, full-bleed, three-screen first-run sequence: track → coach → own your data. Shown once (gated by AppSettings.hasOnboarded) before the athlete ever sees Today. Reuses the same BackgroundView mood-gradient language as the rest of the app rather than introducing a separate onboarding visual style, per the no-cards, full-bleed doctrine.
